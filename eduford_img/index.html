Our Qur’anic Programs

Courses for all ages, focusing on Qur’an recitation, memorization, and Islamic studies with guided, patient learning.”

Qur’an Recitation (Tajweed)

A structured and supportive program that helps students memorize the Qur’an systematically. With personalized guidance and consistent practice, students develop both retention and understanding.

Islamic Studies

Explore the foundations of Islam through lessons in Aqeedah (belief), Fiqh (practical jurisprudence), Seerah (life of the Prophet ﷺ), and daily Islamic practices. This course equips students with knowledge to live a balanced Islamic life.

Arabic for Qur’an Understanding

Learn the basics of Arabic to understand Qur’anic vocabulary, phrases, and meanings. This course strengthens comprehension and enriches the recitation and memorization experience.

Children’s Foundation Class

Designed for young learners, this program introduces Islamic teachings through fun, interactive lessons and activities. It fosters a love for the Qur’an, good character, and essential Islamic values from an early age.

Our Learning Environment

Our learning environment is rooted in kindness, respect, and sincerity. We believe students learn best when they feel safe, valued, and spiritually supported.

Respect and Modesty

We nurture self-respect and modesty in both character and conduct. Students are guided to carry themselves with dignity, confidence, and Islamic manners in all aspects of learning

Sisterhood and Support

Hoor wa Noor encourages a strong sense of sisterhood and mutual care. Students learn together in an atmosphere of cooperation, understanding, and positive peer support.

Compassionate Discipline and Faith

Discipline at Hoor wa Noor is guided by patience and compassion. Through gentle guidance, we help students grow in love for Allah and His words, nurturing both the heart and the mind.
